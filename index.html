Welcome to the GeneHouse Website!

The aim of this project is to clear misconceptions about Genetically Modified Organism (GMO), to introduce verified information about GMO, to educate to educate people about GMO, as well as to get people excited about genetic engineering.

The purpose of this project is not to praise or disregard GMO, but to promote and generate interest about genetic engineering with a hope to enhance scientific researches on this field conducted in the Philippines someday in the future, further expanding the knowledge of the public towards the pros and cons of GMO.

What exactlty is this project?

This project is primarily about teaching about people the pros and cons of Genetically Modified Foods. The project aims to gather 30 (max) persons who are interested at learning about the topic GMO to our place, specifically my friend's, John's house at his yard. Where we will teach how genetic engineering works, and as well as genetically modifying an organism (yeast).

We will be using a projector to display the contents of the topic to be teached on a whiteboard stand.

We will be using a microphone connected to the speaker for all the people to hear.
